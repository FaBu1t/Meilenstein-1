About us

"Moviedatabase" Website is the worlds largest, open accessible and modifyable movie collection and critique forum. Every user can add movies, as they please. And the best: No other user can see these movies. The archive can serve as a guide, inspiration, critique and commentary on all the movies that the site has been hosting. You'll be able to check out some of their other movies!
